Individualised educational therapy based on the NILD (National Institute for Learning Development) approach. This option is designed for learners who experience difficulties with areas such as attention, memory, processing, reading, writing, or mathematics. Therapy sessions focus on strengthening underlying learning skills rather than only addressing academic content.
