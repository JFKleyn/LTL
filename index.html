Learning Options

Our methods of improving your child’s learning experience:

Homework Hub & Workspace

A supervised learning space designed to support learners with daily homework and independent study. For Grades 1–12, this option helps students build good study habits, stay organised, and complete tasks accurately and on time. Work smart, stress less!

Tutoring

Tutor pods of two to four students, ensuring each student receives personalised attention. Sessions are structured, subject-specific and tailored to learners’ needs. The focus is on academic support, concept reinforcement, and exam or test preparation. Practice makes progress!

Homeschool Assistance

Academic support for families who have chosen the homeschooling journey. LTL provides academic guidance and structure. This option assists learners in staying on track with their curriculum requirements. A homely workspace!

Educational Therapy (NILD)

Based on the NILD approach. This option is designed for learners who experience difficulties with areas such as attention, memory, processing, reading, writing, or mathematics. Develop tools for independent learning in the classroom and in life!
